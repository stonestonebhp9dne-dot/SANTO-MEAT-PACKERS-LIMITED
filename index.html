Corporate Profile

QUALITY FROM PASTURE TO PLATE

Santo Meat Packers Limited is an established pillar of Vanuatu's agricultural and export economy. Operating out of Espiritu Santo with corporate offices in Port Vila, we manage a state-of-the-art abattoir and meat processing facility.

Vanuatu beef is renowned globally for being naturally raised, grass-fed, and free from hormones and artificial additives. We process and pack this premium product for both local consumption and international export, maintaining stringent biosecurity and hygiene standards.

  • 100% Grass-Fed Vanuatu Beef

Our Facilities

INDUSTRIAL OPERATIONS

Our processing capabilities are designed to meet the rigorous demands of international trade while supporting local farmers.

Luganville Abattoir

Our primary processing plant is located on Melcoffee Road in Luganville, Espiritu Santo. It serves as the main hub for slaughter, processing, and packaging.

Export Logistics

We manage cold-chain logistics to export high-quality beef and cattle hides to markets including Japan, Australia, Solomon Islands, and Papua New Guinea.

Biosecurity Standards

Working closely with the Vanuatu Biosecurity Department, we ensure all products meet strict health, safety, and hygiene regulations prior to domestic distribution or export.
